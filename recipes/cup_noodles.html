Delicious Cup Noodles

Description

Ingredients

  • Lucky Me La Paz Batchoy
  • water
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil water to make hot water.
  2. Slightly open the slid of the cup noodles.
  3. Open the seasoning and pour it inside the cup.
  4. Pour hot water until 80% of the cup is full.
  5. Close the lid.
  6. Wait for 5 minutes so the noodles can be cooked.
  7. After waiting, open the lid.
  8. Stir and mix the noodles.
  9. Enjoy!
